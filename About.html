About

Have you ever wanted to eat food but there was mold on it? Have you ever had to quickly finish off a certain meal before mold grew on it? Then the Bacto-Stroyer X is the solution for you. Our product is composed of two separate parts: a transmitter and the emitters. The transmitter can receive input from the associated application. With this, the current status and functionality of the associated emitters can be easily and quickly altered by users of this product. The emitter creates ultrasonic frequencies capable of interrupting cell division of bacterial and mold growth in a small radius. The intended implementation of this feature is as an accessory device to any food storage unit. The inhibiting capabilities are intended to be beneficial to the preservation of food within these storage containers.
